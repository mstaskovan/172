Engine Start – Štartovanie motora

  • Master SwitchON
  • Beacon LightON
  • Fuel Shutoff ValveFULL IN
  • Fuel SelectorBOTH
  • MixtureRICH

Priming – Studený motor (ak je potrebné)

  • ThrottleOPEN 1/4 INCH
  • Fuel PumpON
  • MixtureADVANCE UNTIL FUEL FLOW
  • Fuel PumpOFF

Normal Start – Bežné štartovanie

  • ThrottleOPEN 1/4 INCH
  • MixtureRICH
  • Propeller AreaCLEAR
  • Ignition SwitchSTART
  • Oil PressureCHECK

Starting Flooded Engine – Štartovanie zaplaveného motora

  • MixtureCUT-OFF
  • ThrottleFULL OPEN
  • Ignition SwitchSTART
  • MixtureADVANCE WHEN ENGINE STARTS

Before Takeoff – Engine Runup

  • Parking BrakeSET
  • Fuel QuantityCHECK
  • Fuel SelectorVERIFY BOTH
  • MixtureFULL RICH
  • Throttle1,800 RPM
  • Magneto CheckPERFORM
  • → IgnitionR → BOTH → L → BOTH
  • → RPM Drop≤ 150 RPM
  • → RPM Difference≤ 50 RPM
  • Vacuum GaugeCHECK
  • AmpsCHECK
  • AnnunciationsCHECK & CONSIDER
  • Engine InstrumentsCHECK
  • Full Throttle Static RunupPERFORM
  • → Throttle FULL2,300–2,400 RPM
  • → Above 3,000 ftLEAN MIXTURE IF NEEDED
  • ThrottleIDLE or AS REQ
