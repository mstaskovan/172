Preflight - Predletová príprava

  • MSFS EFB – Flight Planning ........ AS DES
  • Pushback & Pre-Positioning ........ AS DES
  • Parking Brake ........ SET
  • Circuit Breakers ........ IN
  • Throttle ........ IDLE
  • Mixture ........ CUT-OFF
  • Alternate Static Air ........ OFF (pushed in)
  • All Electrical Switches ........ OFF
  • Avionics Master ........ OFF
  • Ignition ........ OFF
  • Bat & Alt Master ........ ON
  • Fuel Quantity ........ CHECK
  • Avionics Master 1 ........ ON
  • → verify Avionics Cooling is audible
  • Volts (in Cockpit) ........ CHECK
  • Avionics Master 1 ........ OFF
  • Avionics Master 2 ........ ON
  • → verify Avionics Cooling is audible
  • Avionics Master 2 ........ OFF
  • Annunciator ........ CHECK & CONSIDER
  • Annunciator Switch ........ TEST, CHECK, RELEASE
  • Annunciator Brightness ........ BRT or DIM – AS REC
  • Lights ........ CHECK (if required)
  • Flaps ........ DOWN
  • Pitot Heat ........ ON (30 seconds), then OFF
  • Bat & Alt Master ........ OFF
  • Elevator Trim ........ SET FOR T.O
  • Fuel Shutoff Valve ........ FULL IN
  • Fuel Selector ........ BOTH
